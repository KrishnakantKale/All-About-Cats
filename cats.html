Things cats love:

  • Catnip: It is like a magical herb for felines, sending them into a state of playful frenzy or blissful relaxation with just a whiff.
  • Laser pointer: It is the ultimate tease for cats, triggering their natural hunting instincts as they chase the elusive red dot with endless curiosity and excitement.
  • Lasagna: It might not be on a cat’s usual menu, but its rich, cheesy aroma can make even the most finicky feline curious, as if they’re channeling their inner Garfield.

Top the things cat hate:

  1. Flea treatment
  2. Thunder
  3. Other Cats
